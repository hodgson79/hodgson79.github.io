About

I am a Director of Culture Strategy & Insights at PG&E, building toward becoming the CHRO of the AI era. My work sits at the intersection of organizational culture, enterprise listening, and AI governance — the spaces where human intention meets technological capability.

Over the course of my career, I have developed five branded intellectual property frameworks that form a unified system for transforming how organizations listen, lead, and create value. Each framework stands on its own. Together, they form a discipline.

I believe that AI which does not pay for itself is not AI. That culture is not a slogan but a measurable system. And that authenticity — real, operational authenticity — is the algorithm that makes everything else work.

Domain: The Individual

Core thesis: Authenticity is not a feeling — it is a system. An algorithm of self-awareness, preparation, and intentional action that compounds over time.

Key Components

  • Self-audit

PeoplePowered™
Domain: The Organization

Core thesis: Culture is not a slogan posted on a wall. It is a measurable, operational system that either accelerates or decelerates everything the organization attempts.

Key Components

  • Culture diagnostics
  • Activation architecture
  • Councils and centers of excellence
  • Measurable culture indices

Application

Culture transformation, M&A integration, operational excellence

Voice to Value™
Domain: The Intelligence

Core thesis: Every organization listens. Almost none convert what they hear into measurable value. Voice to Value is the closed-loop architecture that does.

Key Components

  • Relationship listening
  • Pulse listening
  • Experience/transactional listening
  • Lifecycle moments

Application

Enterprise listening strategy, employee experience, customer experience mirroring

ValueAI™
Domain: The Technology

Core thesis: AI that does not pay for itself is not AI. The Three-Engine Paradigm — Return on Employee, Return on Investment, Return on the Future — is the governance standard.

Key Components

  • ROE (human potential optimization)
  • ROI (capital efficiency)
  • ROF (lives saved / frontier value)

Application

AI governance, responsible AI deployment, safety-critical AI in regulated industries

  • Vulnerability as strength

Application

Leadership development, frontline leadership, executive coaching
